Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Kaynko POS ("the App") is a point-of-sale application for Android developed by Kaynko AI (referred to as "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Business information — business name, address, and currency configured during setup.
  • Staff (cashier) accounts — names and hashed PIN codes used for login.
  • Product catalogue — product names, prices, and stock levels you enter.
  • Sales records — sale amounts, payment methods, timestamps, and line items.
  • Customer records — names and phone numbers you enter for credit or wholesale customers.
  • Debt and payment records — amounts owed and payments received for credit customers.
  • Cash session and expense records — opening/closing balances and business expenses.
  • Bluetooth device information — the MAC address of a paired thermal receipt printer, saved in the device's encrypted local storage.

The App does not collect or transmit any information automatically. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or background data uploads of any kind.

2. How Information Is Used

All collected information is used solely to operate the point-of-sale features of the App on your device:

  • Processing and recording sales transactions.
  • Generating receipts and end-of-day reports.
  • Managing customer credit and debt balances.
  • Connecting to a Bluetooth receipt printer.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your Android device in an encrypted SQLite database. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services. Bluetooth printer credentials are stored using Android's Keystore-backed encrypted storage (flutter_secure_storage).

We recommend protecting your device with a screen lock to prevent unauthorised access to the App and the data it contains.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transfer your data to any third party. The App may generate receipt text that you can optionally share via WhatsApp or another messaging app using your device's native share sheet — this sharing is entirely at your discretion and is not initiated by the App automatically.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data persists on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data through your device's application settings. Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data.

To delete a specific record (a product, customer, sale, etc.), use the relevant delete function within the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for use by business owners and their staff. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
